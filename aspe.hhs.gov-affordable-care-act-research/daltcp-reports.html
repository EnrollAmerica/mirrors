Office of Disability, Aging and Long-Term Care Policy (DALTCP): LIST OF REPORTS

This page alphabetically lists reports produced through DALTCP-funded research or through research done by DALTCP staff. (Some reports were funded when DALTCP was known as the Office of Social Services Policy or the Office of Family, Community and Long-Term Care Policy.) Links to the Executive Summaries and/or Full Reports immediately follow each title. Posted First is the date that the first Full Report version (either HTML, PDF or both) was made available online...not when it was first made available from DALTCP. (We are working towards putting ALL versions--both HTML and PDF--of Full Reports online.) [You will need a copy of the Acrobat Reader in order to view PDF versions.] You can also search the entire Office of the Assistant Secretary for Planning and Evaluation website at http://aspe.hhs.gov/_/advanced/index.cfm

Now also available: instruments, questionnaires, etc. developed for projects funded by DALTCP. A list of what is currently available can be viewed at http://aspe.hhs.gov/daltcp/instruments.htm. Instruments, etc. (and links to them) are also listed after each report in its "also available" list. This list was last updated April 13, 2011.

Most reports can be ordered from the Office of Disability, Aging and Long-Term Care Policy. Requests can be made by Fax (202-401-7733) or through email (webmaster.DALTCP@hhs.gov). Please include the ENTIRE report title in your request.
