ACA mirror sites

Archive of data from select U.S. government websites pertaining to the Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act (Public Law No. 111-148) prior to January 20, 2017.
Due to size constraints, raw data scraped from the live websites are stored in Enroll America's S3 bucket; web-browseable mirrors are stored in GitHub Pages and linked-to below.
Please support this data archiving project by creating your own mirrors! Copy some (or all) of these archived websites by following this simple tutorial.
